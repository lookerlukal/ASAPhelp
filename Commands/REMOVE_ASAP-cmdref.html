REMOVE (ASAP Command)

Subtracts best-fit polynomial.

Syntax 

REMOVE [ k ]

OptionDescription
kdegree of best-fit, 1D polynomial (default 1, linear)

Remarks


  1. Removes (subtracts) best-fit, 1D polynomial of degree k (default 1, linear) from distribution data averaged in the other direction.
  2. Use a TRANSPOSE and another REMOVE to subtract a 2D polymomial (excluding any cross terms).
