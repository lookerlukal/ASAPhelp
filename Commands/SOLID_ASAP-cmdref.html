SOLID (ASAP Command)

Makes the previous surface a solid.

Syntax 

SOLID PLUS  [ NOHOLE ]
      MINUS
      OFF

OptionDescription
PLUSuses positive side of surface/function
MINUSuses negative side of surface/function
NOHOLEexcludes the interior hole of the LOCAL box, if one exists
OFFsets the SOLID command to off

Remarks


  1. Make the current surface a SOLID formed from the interior of its LOCAL box and the given side of the function.
  2. Currently, the SOLID command only affects the BOUNDS command.
  3. If the volume needed can be represented by just a LOCAL box, then use the above command with a dummy degree zero surface, for example. 
    SURFACE n
  GENERAL x y z +1
     LOCAL ...
     SOLID PLUS
:
OBJECT
  :
  BOUNDS +-n
