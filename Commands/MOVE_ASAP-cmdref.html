MOVE (ASAP Command)

Moves rays along their propagation direction to a new location.

Syntax 

MOVE BY d
     TO X d
        Y
        Z
        OPL

OptionDescription
BYrelative move
TOabsolute move
ddistance along a given coordinate direction
OPLoptical path length

Remarks


  1. Moves the ray data to a new reference position either BY an amount d (relative move) or TO a position d (absolute move) along the individual ray directions, given coordinate direction, or Optical Path Length (OPL).
  2. During the MOVE operation, the rays are projected along their direction vectors. The rays do not intersect any OBJECTS along the way.
  3. Commonly used for virtual ray tracing from a buried entrance pupil to object space so that the beam may then be traced through the system.
  4. Use to observe the effects of defocus on a beam.
