CRYSTAL (ASAP Command Argument)

Identifies the medium as a birefringent uniaxial crystal.

Syntax 

... [ CRYSTAL a,b,c [ m' ] ]

OptionDescription
a,b,coptical axis direction
m'previous medium

Remarks


  1. The command argument, CRYSTAL identifies the medium as a birefringent uniaxial crystal with optical axis direction a,b,c, and given extraordinary refractive indices.
  2. Ordinary indices are specified on a pervious medium m' (default is last). For example, a calcite crystal with its optical axis initially aligned with the Y direction is defined as follows: 
     MEDIA
 1.66 !ordinary indices for following extraordinary
 1.49 CRYSTAL 0 1 0 =CALCITE
  3. If SPLIT is one or higher, ASAP automatically generates an ordinary and extraordinary ray/beam at each crystal interface.
