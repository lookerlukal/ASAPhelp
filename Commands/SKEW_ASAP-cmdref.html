SKEW (ASAP Command)

Specifies an arbitrary skewing of an entity.

Syntax 

SKEW X a Y [ c ] [ LIST ]
     Z
     Y a Z
     X
     Z a X
     Y

OptionDescription
X Y Zskew direction (first column of X,Y,Z)
aangle measured in degrees from the second direction (second column of X,Y,Z)
ccorresponding coordinate of the skew center in second direction
LISTdecodes transformation matrix into simple operations (if possible) and prints

Remarks


  1. Skews the entity in the first direction by an angle a measured in degrees from the second direction.
  2. The corresponding coordinate of the skew center is optionally given by c. The skew center is defaulted to the entity's reference coordinate.
