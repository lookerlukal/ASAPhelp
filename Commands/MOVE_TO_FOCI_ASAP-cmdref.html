MOVE TO FOCI (ASAP Command)

Moves rays or beams along the propagation direction to a focus with the given parabasal rays and the base ray.

Syntax 

MOVE TO FOCI [ n n' n" ... ]

OptionDescription
n n' n" ...specified parabasal rays

Remarks


  1. Moves each ray/beam to the centroid of the intersections of the given parabasal rays with the base ray.
