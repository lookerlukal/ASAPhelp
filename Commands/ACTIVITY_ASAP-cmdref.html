ACTIVITY (ASAP Command Argument)

Makes the current medium chiral--one that exhibits isotropic optical activity (circular birefringence).

Syntax 

. . .[ ACTIVITY r [ g ] ]

OptionDescription
rradians
gdifference between the right and left-handed refractive indices (see Remarks)

Remarks


  1. For a wavelength w (in system units), the specific rotary power in radians per unit length is,



  2. For r=0, g is just the difference between the right and left-handed refractive indices. Currently, this is assumed to be a purely volume phenomenon and therefore, its (usually small) effect on direction splitting and the reflection/transmission coefficients at a surface are ignored
