WEDGE (ASAP Command)

Creates a wedge of glass.

Syntax 

WEDGE X x h m a [ t ]
      Y y
      Z z

OptionDescription
X or Y or Zglobal coordinate axis
x or y or zlocation on the global coordinate axis
haperture height
mmedium (number or name)
aangle between the two faces
toptional minimum thickness (default is h/10)
