LIST ELLIPSE (ASAP Command)

Lists polarization parameters of currently selected ray data.

Syntax 

LIST ELLIPSE

Remarks


  1. If FRESNEL BOTH is set, prints the major axis orientation (in global coordinates), the ellipticity, and handedness of the polarization ellipse.
  2. The major axis orientation is printed out in global coordinates. The ellipticity ratio is the ratio of the minor to major axis of the polarization ellipses. A purely linear state has a ratio of zero, and a purely circular state has a ratio of 1. The sign of the ellipticity determines the handedness of the polarization ellipse.
