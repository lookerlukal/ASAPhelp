CORNER (ASAP Command)

Creates an axis-aligned corner of a cube (three mutually perpendicular planes), with the apex at the location given by the third entry.

Syntax 

CORNER X x HEIGHT d
       Y y HEXAGONAL
       Z z LENGTH

OptionDescription
HEIGHTcircular corner cube
HEXAGONALsix-sided corner
LENGTHaxial corner cube

Remarks


  1. Creates an axis-aligned corner of a cube (three mutually perpendicular planes) with an apex at the location given by th third entry.
  2. The size of the corner cube can be specified either by a circular HEIGHT, HEXAGONAL height, or an axial LENGTH (maximum cross-section will then be triangular).
  3. Note: A small hole is created at the apex, since the normal to the function becomes undefined there. Therefore, an on-axis ray never hits the surface(s).
