RIGHT (ASAP Command)

Creates a simple prism that deviates the rays by 90 degrees.

Syntax 

RIGHT  X x h m [ Y,Z ]
PENTA  Y y       Z,X
       Z z       X,Y

Option Description
X, Y, or Zglobal coordinate axis
x, y, or zlocation on the global coordinate axis
haperture height
mmedia (name or number)
Y,Z or Z,X or X,Youtput coordinate direction

Remarks


  1. RIGHT and PENTA create the specified 90-degree deviation prism with aperture hight h'. and medium m, and output coordinate direction given by the last entry. RIGHT creates a right-angle prism; whereas, PENTA creates a five-sided prism.
  2. The output coordinate direction establishes the orientation of the prism by specifying the output direction of a ray that originally entered the prism propagating along the input coordinate axis.
  3. All faces are circular.
  4. The best way to model any prism in ASAP is to write a macro that creates a faceted object from two EDGES (see supplied UTIL.LIB).
  5. This lens entity starts out normal to the defined global coordinate axis (X, Y or Z).
