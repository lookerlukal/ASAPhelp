COMPOSITE (Lens Modifier) (ASAP Command)

Combines several lens entities into a single lens entity.

Syntax 

COMPOSITE [   -n    ]
            i [ i']

OptionDescription
nlast n lens entities
i i'range of lens entities

Remarks


  1. Combines the last n lenses, lenses i through i', or all defined lenses since the last LENS command into one lens entity.
  2. Note: IDEAL or PERFECT lens entities are not allowed in a composite set.
