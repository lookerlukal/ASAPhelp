BEND (ASAP Command)

Bends the last surface along a parabolic curve.

Syntax 

BEND X c [ c' ]
     Y
     Z

OptionDescription
X Y or Zaxis which is bent
c c'curvature coefficients of the bending

Remarks


  1. Bends the surface (relative to its reference point) by replacing the given coordinate in its polynomial function with a quadratic in the other two coordinates, for example, for a BEND Z:



  2. The c coefficients are the curvatures (inverse radii) of the bending.
    Note: This operation may double the order of the polynomial.
