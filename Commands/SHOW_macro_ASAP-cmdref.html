$SHOW (ASAP Macro)

Displays the status and states of several internally defined macros.

Syntax 

$SHOW
&SHOW

Remarks


  1. Macro shows the current states of internal macro commands.
  2. The & version also lists the internal stack code (operators/operands) for any $FCN definitions.
