ASCALE (ASAP Command)

Deforms an edge or curve into a keystone.

Syntax 

ASCALE s s'

OptionDescription
sscale factor at the bottom of the edge/curve
s'scale factor at the top of the edge/curve

Remarks


  1. Scales the previous edge along the axis that follows the normal axis of that edge; for example, an ELLIPSE Z is scaled in the x direction.
  2. The bottom and top are determined from the minimum and maximum.
  3. The scaling varies linearly from s at the bottom to s' at the top of the edge (as measured along the third coordinate direction), as shown in the following table where the coefficients a and b are computed from s s' and the height of the edges/curves.

Edges/curves


Coordinate axisScaled axisEquation
XYYscaled = Y (a Z + b)
YZZscaled = Z (a X + b)
ZXXscaled = X (a Y + b)
