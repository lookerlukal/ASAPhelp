SUPERCONIC (ASAP Command)

Superconic asphere used in optical design.

Syntax 

SUPERCONIC X x d c c' c" a b' b" [ aperture... ]
           Y y
           Z z

OptionDescription
dusually one (or zero)
cvertex curvature (inverse radius)
