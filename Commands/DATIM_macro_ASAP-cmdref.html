$DATIM (ASAP Macro)

Switches on and off the printout of date and time in plots.

Syntax 

$DATIM [ ON  [ ON ] ]
         OFF   OFF

Remarks

Turns ON or OFF any date and/or clock time outputs. When turned OFF, the system date and/or time routines return blank strings.
