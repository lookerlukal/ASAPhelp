Any physical BSDF must obey reciprocity; that is, it must be the same when the scatter (U) and specular (W) directions are interchanged. Therefore, the BSDFs dependence on U should be identical to its dependence on W. Unfortunately due to errors, there are a lot of models and even measured data that violate reciprocity.
