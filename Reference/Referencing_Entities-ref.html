Referencing Entities

Databases of surfaces, edges, objects, media (including coatings), rays, and sources may be referenced absolutely or relatively. Objects, media, and coatings also may be referenced literally.


Reference MethodReferencingCommentsFormat
AbsoluteEntity is referenced from the top of the currently defined database.actual entity numbern
RelativeEntity is referenced from the bottom of the currently defined database.max+1-i.I
LiteralMay specify an OBJECT, MEDIA, or COATING An abbreviated form (up to 16 nonblank characters) can be used in place of either absolute or relative referencing.up to 16 character abbreviationname
Note:

  • Forward referencing of undefined entities is not allowed in ASAP.
  • Avoid referencing two (or more) word names literally. ASAP does not necessarily know that a single entity is being referenced.
