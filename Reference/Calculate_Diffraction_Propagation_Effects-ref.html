Calculate Diffraction/Propagation Effects

This topic lists commands that are used when calculating diffraction/propagation effects.


  • A slash between entries indicates that only one of the entries is allowed at a time.
  • Commas indicate that any combination of entries is acceptable.
  • A semi-colon indicates that the commands must be grouped together.

IRRADIANCESet irradiance definition direction
VIOLATIONControls paraxial/positivity/stability messages
SPREAD DIR/POS/APROX/NOR, DOWN, CLIPSpecular/scattered energy distributions
FIELDSUM, DELT, ADD, MULT, COUPLE, CONTCoherent beam summation
FIELDBPM, DELT, MULT, COUPLE, CONTFinite-difference beam propagation
