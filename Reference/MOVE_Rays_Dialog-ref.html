MOVE Rays Dialog

The MOVE Rays dialog, which is accessible from the Rays menu, moves rays along their propagation direction to a new location. This topic describes the fields in the dialog.


MOVE Rays:
By DistanceMoves rays an absolute distance, either along each ray's propagation direction, or along the selected coordinate axis.
To LocationMoves ray data to a new position (absolute) along given coordinate direction.
To PointMoves rays along their propagation direction to their closest approach to the point.
To PlaneMoves rays along their propagation direction to a specified reference plane.
To SphereMoves rays along their propagation direction to a specified sphere.
Absolute MOVE OptionsSpecifies options associated with the selected type of MOVE.
