Coordinate Transformations in ASAP

The coordinate transformations used in ASAP are applicable both to explicit transformations (for example, SHIFT, ROTATE) and implicit transformations (for example, AXIS LOCAL in combination with FIELD or CVF).

The unitary transformation of a point P in the local coordinates of a geometrical entity to a point P’ in global coordinates may be effected by an arbitrary sequence of elementary translations and rotations and as such does not have a unique prescription.
