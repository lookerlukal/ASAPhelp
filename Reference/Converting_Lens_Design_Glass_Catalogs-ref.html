Converting Lens Design Glass Catalogs

The GLA2CAT utility converts a ZEMAX .AGF catalog or an OSLO .GLC glass catalog to an ASAP .CAT format. 

GLA2CAT catalog.AGF
               .GLC

Because underscores (_) have significance in ASAP MEDIA names, they are removed from both the catalog file name and any glass names during conversion.
