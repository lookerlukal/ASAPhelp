CATIA Geometry Filters

CATIA geometry filters can be combined with one another, depending on the filter type.


Filter TypeMain SolidAll SolidsAll Surfaces
Main solidNot applicableNoYes
All solidsNoNot applicableYes
All SurfacesYesYesNot applicable
Note:

Combining the "All Surfaces" filter with any other filter results in a warning:

"Importing a combination of surfaces and solids may result in duplicated surfaces; for example, when importing a surface that was used to construct a solid."
