Incoherent RADIANT Calculation Dialog
Incoherent RADIANT Calculation Dialog

Calculates the far-field incoherent intensity and radiance of selected ray data.


IntensityWhen selected, calculates the far-field incoherent intensity of the currently selected ray data.
Radiance When selected, calculates the far-field incoherent radiance of the currently selected ray data.
Title Enter a title for your plot in the text box.
Polar Axis of Spherical Coordinate System Determines which axis is the polar axis.
Zenith/Latitude Angles From Axis Range in degrees for the angles from this axis. Defaults are 0 to 180 degrees for the angles, and 36 for the subdivisions.
Azimuth/Longitude Angles Around Axis Range in degrees for the angles about the axis. Defaults are -180 to 180 degrees for the angles, and 36 for the subdivisions.
Rescaling Factor An optional rescaling factor for altering the sizes of the polar diagrams relative to the spatial grid spacing.
