FOCUS Dialog

Lists the best 3D focal point and RMS deviations of the current ray data, from this point.


Show Best FocusUses a least-squares-closest approach.
Move Rays to Plane of Best FocusTransfers currently selected ray data to a plane that passes through the best 3D focal point.
Move Rays to Sphere of Radius __ Centered on Best Focus Transfers currently selected ray data to a reference sphere of a specified radius centered on the best 3D focal point.
