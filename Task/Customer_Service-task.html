Customer Service

If you are having a problem with ASAP and are current under the Maintenance Agreement, please contact BRO Technical Customer Service. Contact information is provided in this topic.

Several guidelines that can help BRO Technical Service provide the best service are listed below.


  • Note any error messages that occurred while you were working in ASAP, and what you were doing in ASAP leading up to the error.
  • Check online Help or the ASAP manuals for information concerning the problem or question.

Please provide the following information to Customer Service:


  • Your Breault Customer Identification (select Registration on the Help menu; your ID is the first group of numbers under License Key).
  • Your version of ASAP (select About ASAP on the Help menu).
  • If necessary, send BRO your ASAP input files.
    Note: To speed up the troubleshooting process, please send only the portion of the file that pertains to the problem. Include your Customer ID and the ASAP version number in your message.
Note:

You can expect to receive a response within 24 hours of Customer Service receiving your inquiry. If you do not receive a response to your e-mail or fax within this time, please resend it.
