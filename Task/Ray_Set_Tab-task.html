Ray Set Tab

The BRO 3D Viewer maps the direction sphere to a collection of triangles formed by successive subdivisions of an icosahedron.

Enter the number of spheres needed for the maximum subdivision of rays in the 3D Viewer.

Resulting bins have roughly equal solid angles, typically with more even statistical behavior than bins that are formed from conventional theta-phi division of the direction sphere.
