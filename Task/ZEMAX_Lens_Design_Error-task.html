ZEMAX Lens Design Error

The Lens Design Problem message box displays when the ZEMAX stop surface is buried inside a glass element, which results in an edge violation between the stop surface and a glass surface.


Move stop to front of lensRelocates the stop to be in front of the lens element.
Move stop to back of lensRelocates the stop to be behind the lens element.
IgnoreClick to close the dialog and continue the translation.
CancelClick to cancel the translation and returns to ASAP.
