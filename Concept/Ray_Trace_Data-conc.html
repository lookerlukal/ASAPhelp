Ray Trace Data

The STATS and LIST commands can provide you with specific ray trace information on an OBJECT by OBJECT basis or on a ray-by-ray basis, respectively, which may help you understand the propagation of energy within the opto-mechanical system under analysis.

When you need additional flexibility to isolate specific pieces of ray data not displayed by an ASAP command, and perhaps even to modify that data during or at the end of a ray trace, ASAP has the ability to access ray data information via the GET and PUT commands.


  • GET transfers data pertaining to given ray(s) to internally defined variables.
  • PUT does the opposite.
