Registration Information

the Registration dialog box in ASAP includes your customer ID, keys, and maintenance plan status.

To open the Registration dialog in ASAP, click the Help menu and select Registration. Refer to this dialog for information about your:


  • Customer identification number
  • Hardware and software key identification (ID)
  • ASAP configuration(s)
  • Maintenance support status
    Note: If your maintenance support has expired, please contact your BRO for information on renewing your support.
  • Registration Dialog
    The Registration dialog is accessible from the ASAP Help menu. It includes customer information listed in this topic.
  • Hardware Key
    The SafeNet Sentinel SuperPro or SuperProNet hardware key, included with your ASAP shipment, provides a unique identification number for authorizing software use. ASAP communicates with the Sentinel SuperPro or SuperProNet key to determine if the user is authorized to use the program. The Sentinel SuperPro driver is 'Windows Hardware Quality Labs' certified for Windows® 2000 and Windows® XP (32-bit).
  • Software Keys
    To fully activate your Breault program, enter your software authorization key numbers in the Registration dialog on the Help menu, if they do not already appear in the License Key area.
