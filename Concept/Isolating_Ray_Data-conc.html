Isolating Ray Data

Isolating ray data is an important part of an ASAP analysis. Rays from different sources may be associated with different objects after a ray trace. If you do not isolate rays from the sources of radiation and the object(s) you want to analyze, all rays may be considered for your analysis.

It is a simple task to isolate rays using the commands SELECT and CONSIDER. Use the SELECT command to isolate individual rays or sources, and use the CONSIDER command to isolate rays on objects.
