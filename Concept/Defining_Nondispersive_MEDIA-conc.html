Defining Nondispersive MEDIA

In some situations, you can safely ignore the polychromatic nature of light to perform a valid systems analysis.

For example, many illumination problems fall into this category. In these cases, you do not need to specify a WAVELENGTH to define a medium. Simply create the medium with a single refractive index and a name.
