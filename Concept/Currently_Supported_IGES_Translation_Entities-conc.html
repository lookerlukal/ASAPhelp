Other Restrictions
  • Accepts only fixed ASCII IGES files with default delimiters.
  • By default, translates only visible independent surfaces and related child entities.
  • Model space versions of trimming curves are normally required.
  • Offset surface approximated by simple shift.
  • Trimmed closed or semi-closed surfaces may require manual correction of SWEEP POint for model space (R)BOUNDS curve.
  • Signs of (R)BOUNDS entities for the 143 Bounded Surface are set arbitrarily; 144 Trimmed Surfaces are preferred.
  • No attempt is made to match actual colors.
  • Shell Faces require the /all option, and do not include their bounding loops.
  • Nesting of Subfigures is not allowed.
