GRID Command Overview

The GRID command generates a planar distribution of rays, which, in conjunction with the SOURCE command, creates ray data that can be traced in ASAP.

While there is only one GRID command in ASAP, it includes several options for creating various types of ray distributions:

. . .RECT and . . .ELLIPTIC create rectangular grids of rays within either a rectangular or elliptical outer boundary

. . .POLAR creates various types of polar ray distributions within a circular boundary

. . .HEX creates a hexagonal distribution of rays within a hexagon out boundary

. . .OBJECT creates a distribution of rays located at the center of facets of an EDGE object or on a surface of the LIMITS box of a surface or lens object

. . .WINDOW creates a rectangular raster-type ray grid.

. . .DATA creates a rectangular grid of rays.
