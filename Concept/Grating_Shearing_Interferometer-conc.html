Grating Shearing Interferometer

In the example, Grating Shearing Interferometer Analysis, two pairs of diffraction gratings are placed at the focus of a parabolic mirror under test. Each pair of gratings consists of two gratings with slightly different periods; these are located a short distance apart.

ASAP is instructed to generate +1, -1, and 0 diffracted orders. SPLIT is defaulted to 1 so that the diffracted orders are not additionally diffracted.

When light reflected from the parabolic mirror intersects the gratings, 12 diffracted diverging beams are generated. These beams interfere at the detector plane to create a fringe pattern. The contours of the fringe pattern are an indication of the aberrations caused by the mirror.
