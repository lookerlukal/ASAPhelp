Polychromatic Geometrical MTF of a Doublet

The example shows a geometrical Modulation Transfer Function (MTF) calculation of a doublet. Since it is very well corrected, the geometrical MTF is a bit anomalous at the higher spatial frequencies. For comparison, two focal positions are analyzed: nominal focus (optimized over the entire 1-degree full field of view) and best axial focus.
