Lens Modifiers

Unlike surfaces and edges that have numerous modifiers, only two types of modifiers are supported for lens entities: linear transformations and alteration of specific lens construction information (using the ALTER (Lens Modifier) (ASAP Command).

Tips
  • Not all of the linear transformations are supported by lens entities. For example, a lens entity cannot be scaled or skewed in such a way as to introduce an azimuthal variation in curvature.
  • Lenses entities are restricted to simple conic surfaces; higher order aspherics cannot be applied. However, these restrictions are responsible for one of the main advantages of lens entities in systems modeling: rapid ray tracing.
