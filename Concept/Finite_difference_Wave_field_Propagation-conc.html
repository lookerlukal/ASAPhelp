Finite-difference Wave-field Propagation

ASAP OPTICAL+ uses a unique finite-difference beam-propagation method (FD-BPM) to carry fields into the realm of single-mode fibers, splitters, couplers, and other guided-wave problems.

The ASAP BPM is linked to the same basic geometry, data structures, and analysis tools used throughout the program. This linkage allows relatively easy transitions from propagation of fields through bulk media to small-scale geometric structures.
