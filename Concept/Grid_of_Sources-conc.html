Grid of Sources

In addition to defining grids of rays, ASAP can also define a grid of sources with an extension of the SOURCE command. In this case the grid coordinates become coordinates for sources and not rays. This is useful for setting up a source field for an full-field aberration display (FFAD) or to simulate some types of extended sources for illumination systems. (In most cases, however, the EMITTING commands are more appropriate for extended sources.) The FFAD is essentially an array of spot diagrams for a distribution of field points.
