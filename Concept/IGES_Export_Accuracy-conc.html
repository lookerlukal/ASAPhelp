IGES Export Accuracy

IGES export to ASAP is exact. This topic addresses accuracy issues.

You are limited only by the maximum Bezier order in a particular parametric direction. You can create up to a 10th-order Bezier in one parametric dimension by a 2nd-order Bezier in the orthogonal parametric dimension. ASAP can then simulate up to 20th-order rotationally symmetric Beziers. The Beziers that ASAP creates can be exported exactly as they are to the IGES format.
