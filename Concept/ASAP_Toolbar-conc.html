ASAP Toolbar

The toolbar on the ASAP window provides quick access to some of its most commonly used features. Twenty user-definable buttons are also provided on the Custom Toolbar.

When you place the mouse over a button, a short description of the button, called a ToolTip, is displayed. However, buttons and ToolTips are disabled if their use is not applicable.

To move the Toolbar: Grab the double bar on the Toolbar, and move the Toolbar to a new position, which can be on or off the ASAP window.

To dock the Toolbar: Grab the Toolbar and drag it to the top, left, right, or bottom of the window area. Note: the double bar is not visible when the Toolbar is not docked.
